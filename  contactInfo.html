About Marshal Auto Parts

MARSHAL AUTO PARTS is a leading automotive parts export company, founded in 2009.

We specialize in supplying auto body parts to a global clientele. Our catalog features over 37,000 unique body parts and lighting for European, Japanese & Chinese cars.

Our strategic warehouse location in Changzhou ensures rapid logistics and efficient order processing.

We excel at assembling multi-brand containers tailored to your needs.

Our experienced team ensures quick order turnaround and provides comprehensive support throughout the entire process – from contract signing to shipment.

We handle all necessary documentation and facilitate smooth communication with all partners (agents, carriers)

We guarantee the quality of every shipment.
